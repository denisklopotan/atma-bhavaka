Atma Bhavaka

Ātmā Bhāvaka is a practice that blends the insights of ancient spiritual traditions with modern psychological principles. Its three core elements - intuitive voice expression, spiritual-orientated framework, and intuitive body movement - create a holistic approach to self-exploration and inner alignment (read more under "About").

In comparison with Stemexpressie and Fenix - Voice Expression, this course has a stronger focus on the spiritual dimension of this work and thereby has the potential to be a powerful tool in helping us to re-discover ourselves and strengthen our relationship with Love, God, and Cosmos.

Stemexpressie

What is Stemexpressie?

Stemexpressie (voice-expression) is a playful and liberating way to make your voice sound, give yourself space, and touch deep layers within yourself. The premise is that through sound and expression, you connect more with your inner strength and creativity. Whether you feel tension, shyness, or joy – everything is welcome to be expressed. It is a journey of discovery in sound, where you learn to know and express yourself on a deeper level.

This method is practiced as taught by Marius Engelbrecht, an experienced voice-expression coach from The Hague and one of the teachers of Jarin van Emmichoven. His approach helps you to engage with your voice more freely and authentically, using sound as a powerful means of self-expression and inner liberation.

Basic Course Stemexpressie

This course is a dynamic journey in which you gradually liberate your voice, deepen your expression, and discover what sound does for you. Each evening has its own theme and builds upon the previous session, with room for playfulness, depth, and magic.

Program

First Evening: Discovering and Liberating

  • Introduction and voice warm-up
  • Sound journey and liberating exercises
  • Welcoming tension
  • Group jam session

Second Evening: Rhythm and Jabber

  • Review and deepening
  • Jabber: rhythm and consonants
  • Duo exercise: Jabber story
  • Lead singing-follow singing

Third Evening: The Healing Voice

  • Review and deepening
  • Connecting sounds with the body and emotions
  • Dialogue with the body and feelings
  • Letting healing sounds flow

Fourth Evening: The Song of Your Heart

  • Summary and deepening
  • Solo or duo improvisations
  • Song of your heart
  • Closing: harmony and group singing

Why This Course?

Compared to Atma Bhavaka, the focus here is less on the spiritual aspects. This means it is ideal for people who feel less drawn to spiritual discovery but are still eager to receive the benefits of voice-expression techniques, such as:

  • More self-confidence and expression
  • Deeper connection with yourself and your emotions
  • Discovering playfulness and creativity
  • Connection with others through sound and group singing
